Student Registration System

Designed to improve the efficiency of student enrollment and administration, the Student Registration System provides a convenient way for administrators to input new student details. This includes capturing vital information such as the student's name, date of birth, student ID, roll number, and contact information. The system is crafted to streamline the registration process and facilitate the management of student records, offering a more organized and accessible database
